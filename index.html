OUR MISSION

Education is a right.
Not a privilege.

G.I.F.T.Ed exists to bridge the gap for underfunded schools in the Philippines — providing pens, pencils, notebooks, folders, tablets, and digital tools to students who deserve every opportunity to succeed. Every donation, every event, every conversation moves us closer to a world where no child is held back by a lack of supplies.

Every dollar goes directly to students.

HOW WE HELP

Your donation.
Real impact.

From a few dollars to a full supply kit — every contribution reaches students who need it. Here's exactly how it works.

01

DONATE

You contribute online (internationally) or locally via pickup in North New Jersey. Both Sean and Ruvi can coordinate local pickups if you'd rather donate physical supplies — pens, pencils, notebooks, folders, or tablets — instead of cash.

02

WE SOURCE

Funds are pooled and used to purchase school supplies in bulk. Priority items: pens, pencils, notebooks, folders. Stretch goal: iPads and digitized learning tools for schools with infrastructure to support them.

03

WE DELIVER

Supplies are coordinated for delivery to partner schools in the Philippines, with updates shared across our social platforms and on this website.

04

YOU SEE THE IMPACT

We document and share the impact of every drive — photos, stories, and numbers. Transparency is core to who we are.
